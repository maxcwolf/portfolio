About Me

A full-stack software developer with a background in data management, experienced in working across multi-disciplinary teams, with a passion for constant learning and adept at adapting to new challenges.

Max Wolf

I need to be constantly stimulated with new learning opportunities to thrive. When I started looking into a new career where I could continue to learn and grow, software development with its seemingly infinite amount of possibilities for gaining new skills and further honing your craft, was the perfect next-step for me.

I moved back home to Nashville to pursue my new passion for coding. I attended the Nashville Software School's unique and amazing full-stack developer program focused on simulating a company enviroment using scrum methodology. Rather than just teach me how to regurgitate code, it taught me to work in a collabrative work environment and think critically to overcome challenges. I'm extremely excited to dive into new technologies and begin to master what I already know.

Portfolio

My projects

artMAP

A hybrid mobile app using Ionic and AngularJS frameworks for taking and sharing photos of graffiti and street art. Users authenticated with Firebase can take pictures using their iPhone camera and upload them to Firebase. Geolocation is obtained with a Cordova plugin. Photographs are mapped in realtime using Google Maps API. The app maps all art in your city so you can see where all the best street art is.
